create a poll with multiple options, and a right answer to the question. 

if multiple right answers is specified, undefined behaviours will occur.

flags:
-r
the flag -r indicates the right answer and can be put behind any option

usage in chat (skip /[yourCommand] when directly talking to the bot):
/[yourCommand] multipoll [question?], [option1], [option2], [option3]...

example:
/zoombot multipoll what is the capital of Sweden?, Malmo, Gothenburg, Luleå, Stockholm -r, Umeå
